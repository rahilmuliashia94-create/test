Everest Security Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2009 with a vision to provide world-class security solutions to businesses and homes across India. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in the security industry, serving over 1000+ satisfied clients.

We specialize in bringing advanced Israeli security technology to the Indian market, offering cutting-edge solutions in CCTV surveillance, access control, fire safety, and intrusion detection systems.

Our team of certified security professionals is dedicated to understanding your unique security needs and delivering customized solutions that provide complete peace of mind.

Our Journey

Milestones that define our growth and success

2009 - Foundation

Everest Security was founded in Rajkot, Gujarat with a mission to provide quality security solutions to local businesses.

2012 - Expansion

Expanded operations to major cities in Gujarat and partnered with leading Israeli technology providers.

2015 - ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification, establishing our commitment to quality management systems.

2018 - Pan India Presence

Expanded services across India with offices in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, and Chennai.

2021 - Digital Transformation

Launched cloud-based monitoring solutions and AI-powered analytics for enhanced security.

2024 - Industry Leader

Recognized as one of India's top security solutions providers with 5000+ successful projects.
