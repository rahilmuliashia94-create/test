Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I get a quote for my security needs?

You can get a free quote by filling out the contact form on this page, calling us at +91 9624696247, or emailing us at sales@everestsecurityonline.com. Our team will schedule a site survey and provide a detailed proposal within 48 hours.

What areas do you serve?

We have offices in major cities across India including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, and our headquarters in Rajkot, Gujarat. We provide pan-India service coverage for all our clients.

Do you provide warranty on products?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranty ranging from 1 to 3 years depending on the product. We also offer extended warranty options and comprehensive AMC packages for complete peace of mind.

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies based on project size. A typical home installation takes 1-2 days, while commercial projects may take 1-4 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal stage and work to minimize disruption to your operations.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we have tie-ups with leading financial institutions to offer EMI options for our clients. We also offer rental options for certain products. Contact our sales team for more details on financing options.
